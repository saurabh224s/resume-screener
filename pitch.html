HireIQ
AI Resume Screener

An intelligent automation system that screens, scores, and ranks candidates against any job description — in seconds.

Category: Open Innovation
Type: AI Automation System
Tech: Claude AI + Python + Flask
The Problem

Manual resume screening is broken

Every job posting receives dozens to hundreds of resumes. HR teams spend hours reading each one manually — a slow, inconsistent, and exhausting process that delays hiring and introduces bias.

23h
Average time spent screening resumes per role
250+
Resumes received per corporate job opening
88%
Of HR professionals say screening is their biggest bottleneck
The Solution

AI that screens faster than any human

HireIQ uses Claude AI to instantly analyze each resume against the job description — extracting skills, scoring match quality, and ranking every candidate automatically.

🎯
AI Match Scoring
Each resume receives a 0–100 match score based on skills, experience, and alignment with the job requirements.
🔍
Skills Gap Analysis
Identifies which required skills each candidate has — and which ones they're missing.
📊
Instant Ranking
Candidates are automatically ranked from best to worst match, saving hours of comparison work.
📧
Email Reports
Sends a beautifully formatted shortlist report directly to the HR team's inbox after every analysis.
How It Works

4 steps, seconds to complete

1
Paste the Job Description
HR enters the job title and full description — required skills, responsibilities, and qualifications.
2
Upload PDF Resumes
Drag & drop up to 10 PDF resumes at once. The system extracts all text automatically using pdfplumber.
3
Claude AI Analyzes Each Resume
Each resume is sent to Claude AI with the job description. Claude returns a match score, top skills, missing skills, summary, and a Shortlist / Maybe / Reject recommendation.
4
Ranked Results + Email Report
Candidates appear ranked by score with full breakdowns. Optionally, a formatted report is emailed directly to the HR team.
